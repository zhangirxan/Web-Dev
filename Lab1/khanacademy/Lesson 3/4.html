Recipe #1

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: Lazy Khachapuri
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 200 ml
Egg 1 pcs
Flour 160 g
Salt 1 g
Baking Powder 1 g
Any cheese 200-300 g

Step 1:Beat eggs and add salt.

Step 2: Add flour and baking powder to the egg mixture.

Step 3: Add grated cheese to the dough and stir.

Step 4: Bake adding a drop of oil in a frying pan for 5-7 minutes on each side.

Recipe #2

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: Easy Pizza
Ingredients Quantity
Pita 2 pcs
Sausage or another meat product 2-3 pcs
Any Sauce 10 g
Tomatos 1 pcs
Any cheese 200-300 g

Step 1:Сut the tomatoes and meat as you like.

Step 2: Grate the cheese.

Step 3: Put grated cheese to first pita and cover with the second pita.

Step 4:Spread the sauce on the second pita.

Step 5:Put the tomatos and meat to pita, then with remaining cheese sprinkle the our "pizza".

Step 6:Bake 5 minutes in oveт or frying pan w/o any oil.
